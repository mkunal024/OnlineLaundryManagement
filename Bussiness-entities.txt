Business Entities :

1. Customer :
      Customer ID, Customer name,Customer Mobile no., Customer Email, Customer Address.
2. Laundry Owner: 
      Laundry ID, Owner name, Owner address, Owner email, Owner contact, Laundry name
3. Laundry Employee:
      Employee ID, Employee Name, Employee address, Employee salary, Employee contact 
4. Delivery Staff: 
      Delivery staff name, Delivery Type(pickup/drop), Delivery ID
5. Services: 
      Service ID, Services name, Service price
6. Order Status: 
      Order ID, Order Quantity, Order status
7. Placed order: 
      Order weight,order amount, payment details, payment ID
